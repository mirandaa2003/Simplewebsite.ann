Vitamins in a Carrot Shake

1. Vitamin A (Beta-Carotene)

Carrots are very high in beta-carotene, which the body converts to Vitamin A.

Benefits: Good eyesight, glowing skin, stronger immunity.

2. Vitamin C

Helps boost the immune system.

Supports healing and healthy skin.

3. Vitamin K

Needed for normal blood clotting.

Supports bone health.

4. Vitamin B6

Helps convert food into energy.

Good for brain and nerve function.
